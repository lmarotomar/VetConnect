## 2. GENERAL RESTRICTIONS

You may not use the Service to:

### 2.1 Illegal Activities
- Violate any applicable local, state, national, or international law
- Promote illegal activities
- Infringe intellectual property rights (copyright, trademark, etc.)
- Distribute child pornography or non-consensual sexual content

### 2.2 Security
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access to the Service or other accounts
- Interfere with or disrupt the integrity or performance of the Service
- Run vulnerability scans, penetration tests, or DDoS attacks without authorization
- Distribute malware, viruses, trojan horses, or other harmful code
- Use phishing techniques to obtain credentials

### 2.3 Abuse / Spam
- Send unsolicited communications (spam) via email, SMS, or WhatsApp
- Send messages to people who have not consented to receive them
- Use the Service for mass marketing unrelated to your veterinary clinic
- Impersonate another person or entity

### 2.4 Content
- Upload content that is defamatory, obscene, harassing, threatening, or hateful
- Promote discrimination based on race, gender, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, or age
- Upload content that invades the privacy of others

## 3. SPECIFIC USE LIMITS

### 3.1 WhatsApp Business API
If you use the WhatsApp integration, you must comply with:
- [WhatsApp Business Policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/business-policy/)
- [WhatsApp Commerce Policy](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/commerce-policy/)

Specific prohibitions:
- Sending messages outside the 24-hour window without a template
- Sending promotional content without opt-in
- Using WhatsApp to sell prohibited items (drugs, animals, etc.)

### 3.2 SMS (Twilio)
- You may not send SMS to numbers on "Do Not Call" lists
- You must honor requests to stop receiving messages (STOP)
- You may not use generic or misleading sender IDs

## 4. FAIR USE

### 4.1 Resource Usage
VetConnect is a shared multi-tenant environment. You may not:
- Consume excessive resources (CPU, memory, storage) that affect other users
- Perform automated requests at a rate that exceeds our API limits
- Use the Service for crypto-mining

### 4.2 Storage
- Storage is for files related to the veterinary clinic (records, images, invoices)
- You may not use the Service as a generic file repository (e.g., storing movies, non-work software)
