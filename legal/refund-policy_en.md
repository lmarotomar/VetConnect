# REFUND POLICY
## VetConnect

**Last Updated:** December 7, 2025

---

## 1. GENERAL PRINCIPLE

At VetConnect, we strive to provide a high-quality service. However, we understand that circumstances may change. This Policy describes our conditions for refunds.

---

## 2. MONTHLY SUBSCRIPTIONS

### 2.1 No Refunds for Partial Months
If you cancel a monthly subscription, you will not receive a refund for the current month. Access to the Service will continue until the end of the billing cycle.

### 2.2 48-Hour Exception
If you were charged by mistake or forgot to cancel, we offer a refund window of **48 hours** after the charge. Contact support immediately.

---

## 3. ANNUAL SUBSCRIPTIONS

### 3.1 30-Day Guarantee
If you cancel an annual subscription within the first **30 days**, we will issue a full refund (100%).

### 3.2 Post-30 Days
After the first 30 days, annual subscriptions are generally non-refundable.
- In exceptional cases (long-term service failure), we may offer a prorated refund for unused months, at our sole discretion.

---

## 4. HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

**Step 1:** Cancel your subscription in the system to prevent future charges.

**Step 2:** Send an email to billing@vetconnect.com with:
- Subject: Refund Request
- Account Email
- Reason for refund
- Date of charge

**Step 3:** We will review your case within 3-5 business days.

---

## 5. REJECTED REFUNDS

We reserve the right to deny refunds if:
- We detect abuse of this policy
- The account was terminated for violation of Terms of Service (spam, fraud, illegal use)
- The request is made outside the established timeframes

---

## 6. PROCESSING TIME

Once approved, the refund will be processed immediately by us. However, it may take **5-10 business days** to appear on your bank statement, depending on your bank.
