## 1. Introduction

VetConnect ("we", "our", or "us") uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and application. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding cookies.

## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, making your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

### Types of Cookies We Use:

#### 2.1 Essential Cookies
**Purpose:** Required for the website to function properly.  
**Duration:** Session or Persistent  
**Examples:**
- Authentication cookies (keep you logged in)
- Security cookies (prevent fraud)
- Load balancing cookies (distribute traffic)

**These cookies cannot be disabled** as they are necessary for the service.

#### 2.2 Performance Cookies
**Purpose:** Collect information about how visitors use our website.  
**Duration:** Persistent (up to 2 years)  
**Examples:**
- Google Analytics cookies
- Page load time tracking
- Error logging

**Data collected is anonymous** and used to improve website performance.

#### 2.3 Functional Cookies
**Purpose:** Remember your preferences and choices.  
**Duration:** Persistent (up to 1 year)  
**Examples:**
- Language preference
- Theme selection (dark/light mode)
- Dashboard customization
- Timezone settings

#### 2.4 Targeting/Advertising Cookies (Third-Party)
**Purpose:** Deliver relevant advertisements.  
**Duration:** Persistent (up to 2 years)  
**Examples:**
- Google Ads cookies
- Facebook Pixel
- LinkedIn Insight Tag

**Note:** We only use these if you have a Premium or Enterprise account and have opted in.

## 4. Local Storage and Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we use:

### Local Storage
- Stores application state
- Caches data for offline functionality
- Never used for tracking

### Session Storage
- Temporary data for current session
- Cleared when you close the browser
- Used for form auto-save

### IndexedDB
- Stores larger amounts of data locally
- Used for offline mode in our PWA
- Data stays on your device only

## 5. Your Cookie Choices

### Managing Cookies

#### Option 1: Cookie Consent Banner
When you first visit VetConnect, you'll see a cookie consent banner with options:
- **Accept All:** All cookies enabled
- **Essential Only:** Only necessary cookies
- **Customize:** Choose which categories to enable

#### Option 2: Browser Settings
Most browsers allow you to:
- Block all cookies
- Block third-party cookies only
- Delete cookies after each session
- Receive a warning before accepting cookies

**How to manage cookies in popular browsers:**
- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings > Privacy > Cookies

#### Option 3: VetConnect Settings
In your account settings, go to:
**Settings > Privacy > Cookie Preferences**

You can toggle:
- ✅ Essential Cookies (always on)
- ⚪ Performance Cookies
- ⚪ Functional Cookies  
- ⚪ Advertising Cookies

### Opt-Out Links

**Google Analytics:** [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)  
**Facebook Ads:** [Facebook Ad Preferences](https://www.facebook.com/ads/preferences)  
**LinkedIn Ads:** [LinkedIn Ad Settings](https://www.linkedin.com/psettings/advertising)

## 6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

### Essential Cookies Disabled
⚠️ The website **will not function** properly:
- Cannot log in
- Cannot save settings
- Security features compromised

### Performance Cookies Disabled
- We cannot improve the website based on usage data
- Error tracking may not work
- Some features may load slower

### Functional Cookies Disabled
- Language preference not remembered
- Theme resets to default
- Dashboard customizations lost

### Advertising Cookies Disabled
- You will still see ads (if applicable)
- Ads will be less relevant to you
- May see the same ad repeatedly

## 7. Do Not Track (DNT)

We respect the "Do Not Track" browser setting. If you enable DNT:
- We will not use performance cookies
- We will not use advertising cookies
- We will only use essential and functional cookies

**To enable DNT:**
- Chrome/Edge: Settings > Privacy > Send "Do Not Track"
- Firefox: Settings > Privacy > Send "Do Not Track"
- Safari: Automatically honors DNT
