# SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
## VetConnect Premium

**Effective Date:** December 7, 2025

---

## 1. COMMITMENT

This SLA applies only to customers subscribed to the **Premium Plan**.

VetConnect commits to using commercially reasonable efforts to make the Service available with a **Monthly Uptime Percentage** of at least **99.9%** during any monthly billing cycle.

---

## 2. DEFINITIONS

- **"Downtime"**: Period during which the Service is unavailable to the Customer (returns 5xx errors or connection timeout).
- **"Monthly Uptime Percentage"**: Calculated by subtracting from 100% the percentage of minutes during the month in which the Service was unavailable.
- **"Maintenance"**: Scheduled maintenance with at least 24 hours prior notice. Maintenance is NOT unexpected Downtime.

---

## 3. SERVICE CREDITS

If we do not meet the commitment, you will be eligible for a Service Credit:

| Monthly Uptime Percentage | Service Credit |
|---------------------------|---------------|
| Less than 99.9% but >= 99.0% | 10% of monthly fee |
| Less than 99.0% but >= 95.0% | 25% of monthly fee |
| Less than 95.0% | 100% of monthly fee |

*Credits are applied to the next bill.*

---

## 4. EXCLUSIONS

The SLA does not apply to interruptions caused by:
- Force Majeure (natural disasters, war, acts of terrorism)
- Failures of the user's Internet provider
- Issues with user equipment/software
- Failures in third-party services (AWS outage, WhatsApp down, Stripe down)
- Suspension of account for Violation of Terms

---

## 5. CLAIMS PROCESS

To request a credit:
1. Contact support@vetconnect.com within 30 days of the incident.
2. Include dates and times of unavailability.
3. We will verify with our system logs.
